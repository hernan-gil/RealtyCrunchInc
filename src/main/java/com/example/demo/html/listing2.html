Description

Closings early 2020! Enter directly into a gracious foyer in this generous 2 bed, 2.5 bath residence. Through the gallery, the charming living and dining room is graceful and inviting, with high ceilings and oversized windows offering lots of light and charming streetscape tree-top views of West 74th Street. The kitchen, which is both tucked away and open to the living space, is the perfect blend of functionality and beautiful design, with state-of-the-art appliances, handcrafted cabinetry, and gorgeous... Carrara marble countertops and backsplash. There is also a powder room located right off the main living space.


The large master suite is a peaceful oasis, with plenty of space, charming exposures, two oversized closets, and an elegant five-piece master bathroom designed with a custom walnut vanity, Blue De Savoie walls, and heated Calacatta marble floors. The secondary bedroom is also spacious and bright and has its own full bathroom.


Additionally, the residence has a top of the line VRF heating and cooling system and is fully wired with Lutron lighting system. The windows are wired with electric for shades and there is a full-sized vented washer/dryer, and a private storage unit.


The Marbury, located at 164 West 74th Street, is a boutique new conversion that has been thoughtfully designed for luxurious, easy and practical living. The beautifully restored faade alone will steal your heart, but thats just the beginning. The residences are both classic and contemporary, with high ceilings, elegant finishes and large windows displaying beautiful tree lined views on lower floors and wide-open city views higher up. With 14 residences, including two penthouses and two garden units, The Marbury is a boutique condominium with the services and amenities one might find in a larger building, including an attended lobby, a landscaped and furnished garden, a state-of-the-art fitness center, a residents lounge with wine storage, a large wood-paneled private dining room that uniquely converts to a billiards room, and a bicycle room. Situated on one of the most charming tree-lined Upper West Side blocks, The Marbury is nestled between Central Park and Riverside Park and is surrounded by some of the neighborhoods best dining, shopping and cultural institutions.
